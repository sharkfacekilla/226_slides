Under Quizzes, there is a new quiz titled Sample TCP Quiz.  The questions are the same as the ones on the TCP slide set.  On completion, D2L will evaluate your answers and provide corrections for any mistake(s).  This is for practice only, not for marks.

The real exam is graded by a human, not D2L; so don't worry if you lost marks on the Sample TCP Quiz due to formatting, similar words (e.g., and vs & vs +), etc.
